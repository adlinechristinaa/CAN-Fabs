Since 1991

Crafting Architectural Excellence

Officially founded in 2005 (with conceptual roots dating back to 1991), Can Fabs is located in Koundampalayam, Coimbatore. Under the premium guidance of our owner, Nithyakumar, we serve both commercial and residential clients with top-tier fabrication and glass solutions.

Over three decades, we have defined our reputation through meticulous precision engineering, timely project execution, and premium high-quality materials. Our highly skilled workforce handles the most complex structural challenges with elegance.

Concept Conceived
